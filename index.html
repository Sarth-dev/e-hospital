Patient Registration (OPD,Casuality,Appointment & ORS)

 
                The Patient registration module of the e-hospital 
                     application is used for the patient registration in the 
                            OPD and casualty department as well as to book,confirm 
            and cancel appointment.

Admission,Discharge & Transfer(IPD)

 
                        The IPD module commences when the patient is being
                     registered and allotted bed in the ward.it deals with
                            complete treatment and services provided to the patient
        during his stay in the hospital.

Billing

 
                The billing module handle all type billing workflows
                This module facilitates cashier and billing operators
            For managing billing functions related to billing 
receipts and refunds.

Clinic(OPD & IPD)

 
                The clinic module allows the clinician and doctors to
        Record the clinical data of the patients like visit,
                   Examination, diagnosis, history, treatment, prescription
                      etc., and to order investigation, procedure and medicine,
                        to keep track of the treatment and other services provided
to the patients.
